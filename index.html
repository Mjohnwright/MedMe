About Med ME

Dosage Maintenance and Administration

At MedME we manage your DRUGS. Enter the information from your pharmacy's prescription label and we will create a medicinal schedule showing a picture of your tablet, your medication name, your dosage amount and the time that you are to take the medicine. We'll alert you of any known drug interactions between your prescriptions and any known side effects. The great part of Medme is that we remind you of when you need to "take your meds" and at what doseage by a text or email. Great for those who care for the elderly. We can help you find a pharmacy near you.
